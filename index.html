About Me & Research

Hi, I'm Hanna — an Information Manager and Researcher with a strong background in research, due diligence, information management, and OSINT analysis. I've worked across academic and corporate environments, including Jagiellonian University, ALDI Tech Hub, and Eurofins, where I honed my skills in navigating complex information systems, building workflows, and knowledge management frameworks.

About Thesis: Within the scope of my PhD, I investigated the information management processes related to air quality in Polish metropolises. I analyzed how it is produced from raw data to the final message, how it is presented in urban spaces, and the information environment of residents (Kraków, Katowice, Wrocław, Łódź, Szczecin, Gdańsk). This study supports the achievement of UN Agenda 2030 Sustainable Development Goals: 9.5, 9.C, 13.2, 13.3, 16.1, and 16.10.

When I’m not working, you’ll likely find me by the sea with a book in one hand, making new music with Glicol, and a perfectly brewed flat white in the other.
