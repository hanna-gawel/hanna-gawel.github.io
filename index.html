About

Hi, I'm Hanna — an Information Manager and Researcher with a strong background in research, due diligence, information management, and OSINT analysis. I've worked across academic and corporate environments (Jagiellonian University, ALDI Tech Hub, Eurofins) navigating complex information systems and building Power Platform workflows.

PhD Thesis: Information Management on Air Quality in Polish Metropolises in Light of Information Ecology. Investigated how information is produced from raw data to final message and managed within the information environment. Connected to UN Agenda 2030 SDGs: 9.5, 9.C, 13.2, 13.3, 16.1, 16.10.
