Bio & Research

Currently working as 🔍 Research Assistant at Jagiellonian University in Institute of Information Studies.

Hi, I'm Hanna — an Information Manager and Researcher with a strong background in research, due diligence, information management, and OSINT analysis. I've worked across academic and corporate environments, including Jagiellonian University and Shell Business Operations, where I honed my skills in navigating complex information systems and building knowledge management frameworks.

My teaching focuses on empowering students to search and think differently in the digital world. I specialize in studying internet phenomena such as content moderation tools, Web3 ecosystems, online communities, information ecology, and the growing role of low-code/no-code development.

PhD Thesis

Information Management on Air Quality in Polish Metropolises in Light of Information Ecology
RUJ repository

About Thesis: Within the scope of my PhD, I am investigating the information management processes related to air quality in Polish metropolises - how it is produced from the raw data to the final message, how it is presented in the urban space and the information environment of the resident of Kraków, Katowice, Wrocław, Łódź, Szczecin and Gdańsk, and how it is managed about the information ecology.

This study is part of the achievement of the objectives: 9.5, 9.C, 13.2, 13.3 and 16.1, 16.10 of the listed 17 Sustainable Development Goals contained in Agenda 2030.
