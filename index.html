Bio & Research

Research Focus: communication, media studies, information ecology, information environment.

Note: Currently working as 🔍 Research Assistant at Jagiellonian University in Institute of Information Studies.

Hi, I'm Hanna — an Information Manager and Researcher with a strong background in research, due diligence, information management, and OSINT analysis. I've worked across academic and corporate environments, including Jagiellonian University and Shell Business Operations, where I honed my skills in navigating complex information systems and building knowledge management frameworks.

My teaching focuses on empowering students to search and think differently in the digital world — uncovering meaningful insights, identifying high-quality information, and making authentic connections. I specialize in studying internet phenomena such as content moderation tools, Web3 ecosystems, online communities, information ecology, and the growing role of low-code/no-code development. Previously, I’ve contributed to customer operations, database design, and the development of knowledge models.

When I’m not working, you’ll likely find me by the sea with a book in one hand and a perfectly brewed flat white in the other. If you’re looking for a motivated, research-driven collaborator with a passion for data-informed innovation, let’s connect!
