Research Project -"Information regarding air quality in Poland vs. information ecology" - PhD Dissertation (2019-2022) [ENG]

The main objective of this project in the scope of theory is a discursive construction of the optimized infoecological information management model regarding air quality. In the scope of methodology, the main objective of the study is an improvement of the methodology of studying information management models, as basic analytical units in information management studies, using mixed research methods and techniques. It strengthens the new conceptual approach to analyzing information management models, which draws attention to solutions used in cities, information needs of residents, and finally, the shape and form of final messages for the recipient. The research itself, due to its multidisciplinary character, can be perceived as an awareness action, helping to understand the need to raise awareness and civic sense of access to public information.
