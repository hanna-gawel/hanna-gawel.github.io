Dr. Hanna Gaweł

Researcher at Jagiellonian University exploring the intersections of information ecology, digital resistance, and counterculture media in the digital age.

Google Scholar ORCID ResearchGate Bluesky OpenReview Beacons.ai
Current Focus
🏛️ Research Assistant at Jagiellonian University (Institute of Information Studies)

My research focuses on the management of information within pirate radio and underground journalism, specifically how these communities navigate the digital age. Methodologically, I seek to bridge qualitative media studies with computational and AI-driven research to analyze digital trace data, AI-driven misinformation, and post-truth media environments. I also investigate the strategic intersection of knowledge management and automation via low-code platforms.
