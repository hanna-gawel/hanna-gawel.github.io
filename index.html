Dr. Hanna Gaweł

Researcher at Jagiellonian University exploring the intersections of information ecology, digital resistance, and the evolution of counterculture media in the digital age.

Current Focus
🏛️ Research Assistant at the Jagiellonian University (Institute of Information Studies)

My research focuses on the management of information within pirate radio and underground journalism, specifically how these communities navigate the digital age. Methodologically, I seek to bridge qualitative media studies with computational and AI-driven research to analyze digital trace data, AI-driven misinformation, and post-truth media environments. Additionally, I investigate the strategic intersection of knowledge management and automation, specifically how low-code/no-code platforms and DevOps frameworks can transform organisational information systems.
